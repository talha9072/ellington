Guided by innovation and a vision for the future, Emaar continues to deliver luxury master-planned communities with unmatched quality and world-class amenities across the globe.

Our Journey

Emaar’s History & Milestones

1997

Emaar Properties is founded in Dubai, setting out with a vision to transform the real estate landscape of the UAE.

1999

Launch of Dubai Marina, the world’s largest man-made marina — a benchmark for luxury waterfront living.

2004

Groundbreaking of Burj Khalifa and The Dubai Mall, setting the stage for two of the most iconic landmarks in the world.

2010

Opening of Burj Khalifa — the tallest tower globally — redefining Dubai’s skyline and cementing Emaar’s global reputation.

Today

Emaar stands as a global leader in real estate with developments across the Middle East, North Africa, Asia, Europe, and North America — trusted by millions worldwide.
